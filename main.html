3 Idiots is a comedy-drama film that follows the lives of three engineering students. The movie highlights the importance of creativity, passion, and learning beyond academic pressure while delivering a strong social message.

Actors

The main actors in the movie are Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, Sharman Joshi, Kareena Kapoor, and Boman Irani.
