Summary

"3 Idiots" follows the journey of three engineering students who challenge the traditional education system. The movie highlights friendship, innovation, and the importance of pursuing one's dreams instead of blindly following societal expectations.

Actors

Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, Sharman Joshi, Kareena Kapoor Khan, and Boman Irani play the major roles in the movie.
